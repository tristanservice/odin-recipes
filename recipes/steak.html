South African Braai Steak

Doesn't that look juicy!

Ingredients

  • Steak & chops seasoning mix.
  • Steak sauce.
  • Grass-fed beef steak.

Steps

  1. Prepare the Steak

    2. Apply steak spices. It is best to use a steak & chops seasoning mix.

    Alternatively, use: Salt, Pepper, Coriander, Paprika, Garlic, Chilli, Nutmeg, Sage and/or Thyme

  2. Prepare the braai

    3. The Braai should be on medium-hot coals at this point.

    Optional: lock the steak in a braai-grid.

  3. Cook to doneness

    4. Apply steak sauce during the last minute.

    Flip every 30 seconds.

    • Rare: 2 minutes
    • Medium Rare: 4 minutes
    • Medium Well: 7 minutes
    • Well done: 9 minutes

  4. Rest and serve

    5. Enjoy your steak!
